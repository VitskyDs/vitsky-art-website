# Footer newsletter signup

Build an email list from day one to notify subscribers about new works, exhibitions, and commission availability.

## Steps

- Add a newsletter signup form to the site footer (email input + subscribe button)
- Integrate with Mailchimp or Brevo free tier (if Brevo is already chosen for transactional email in task-021, use the same platform)
- On submit: add the email to the list via the provider's API and show a Hebrew success message
- Keep the form minimal and non-intrusive — a single email field with a small CTA is enough
- Optional: subtle banner variant for homepage if the footer form has low visibility

## Notes

- No double opt-in is strictly required under Israeli law, but it's good practice and reduces spam complaints
